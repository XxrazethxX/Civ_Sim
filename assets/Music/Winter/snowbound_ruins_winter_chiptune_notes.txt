SNOWBOUND RUINS — ORIGINAL WINTER DARK-FANTASY CHIPTUNE
===========================================================

Core identity:
- Deliberately different from the earlier fast dark-fantasy test.
- This is a slow 6/8 snowfall march, not a battle loop.
- It prioritizes emptiness, cold air, distant ruins, and survival in winter.

Musical details:
- Tempo: 72 BPM (dotted-quarter pulse)
- Meter: 6/8
- Key center: E minor, with D# tension on B-major turns
- Length: 24 bars / approximately 40 seconds

Sound palette:
- Soft metallic ice-bell lead
- Narrow pulse-wave frozen harp
- Triangle-wave low drone
- Wind-like filtered noise
- Irregular snow-crunch texture instead of a normal drum beat

Use case:
Winter seasonal background music for a dark-fantasy kingdom/idle game.
It is intentionally bleak and atmospheric—not festive, cozy, or cheerful.

Original composition. No sampled audio or copied melody is used.
