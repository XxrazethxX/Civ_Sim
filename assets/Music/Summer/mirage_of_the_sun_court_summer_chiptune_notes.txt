MIRAGE OF THE SUN COURT — DARK-FANTASY SUMMER CHIPTUNE
========================================================

Direction:
A more explicitly desert / Middle Eastern-inspired summer theme, written as
an original chiptune rather than a copy of a specific regional or game track.

Musical choices:
- Tempo: 94 BPM
- Meter: 7/8 grouped 3+2+2
- Scale color: D Hijaz / Phrygian-dominant-inspired palette
  (D, Eb, F#, G, A, Bb, C)
- Continuous oud-like plucked ostinato
- Breathier ney-like melody centered on the Eb -> F# Hijaz movement
- Synthesized darbuka-style doum/tek percussion
- Low desert-wind texture and dry, warm echo

Length:
16 bars / approximately 71.5 seconds

This is an original composition and does not sample or reproduce another
song or soundtrack.
