THORNBLOOM DAWN — ORIGINAL DARK-FANTASY SPRING CHIPTUNE
==========================================================

Seasonal direction:
Spring in a dark-fantasy kingdom: wet forests, roots pushing through old
battlefields, faint dawn light, and something dangerous waking under the soil.

How it differs from the other seasonal tracks:
- Winter: slow 6/8, isolation, wind, ice bells.
- Fall: dark harvest procession, wooden pulse, dry leaves.
- Spring: 96 BPM, climbing melody, rain-drop tones, living root bass,
  and damp forest echoes.

Music:
- Tempo: 96 BPM
- Meter: 4/4
- Key color: A Dorian (minor with a lifted F#)
- Length: 20 bars / approximately 50 seconds

Palette:
- Reed-like lead melody
- Triangle-wave root bass
- Narrow pulse-wave climbing arpeggios
- Soft earth thumps
- Rain/dew ping tones and wet-noise ambience
- Sparse eerie bells

This is an original composition and does not sample or reproduce another
game's melody or soundtrack.
